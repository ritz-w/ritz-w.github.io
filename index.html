Programming and the concept of SELF
23 July 2018

This week I went to an exhibition at the Hayward Gallery, they were showing a retrospective of Korean artist Lee Bul's work. The work in the last room was called Via Negativa, which consisted of a maze of fragmented mirrors leading to a central room with an infinity mirror chamber, surrounded by lights. People really did like taking selfies in this work. The outside of the structure is pages printed in reverse of Julian Jaynes' 'The origin of consciousness.'
